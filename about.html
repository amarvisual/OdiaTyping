About Us

Our mission to promote Odia language through digital typing education

Who We Are

ଓଡ଼ିଆ ଟାଇପିଂ ଶିକ୍ଷା (Odia Typing Shiksha) is a free online platform dedicated to teaching Odia typing to students, professionals and language enthusiasts. We believe every Odia speaker should express themselves digitally in their mother tongue.

Our platform uses the popular Akruti English Phonetic keyboard layout — the standard used in government offices, newspapers and media houses across Odisha. No software installation needed.

🎯

Mission

Make Odia typing accessible to everyone — students, professionals, senior citizens — completely free, no barriers.

🌐

Digital Odia

Promote Odia script in digital communication — emails, social media, government documents and more.

📚

Progressive

36 carefully designed lessons from basic vowels to full paragraphs about Odia culture, history and wisdom.

🆓

Always Free

All 36 lessons, all features, progress tracking — completely free, no registration ever required.

Built with Pride in Odisha

This platform is built by people who love Odia culture, the Jagannath tradition, Odissi dance, Pattachitra art and the rich heritage of our state. We dedicate this to every Odia speaker who wants to type their mother tongue with speed and confidence.
