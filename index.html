Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about learning Odia typing

Do I need to install Akruti software?
No! Our website converts your regular keyboard input to Odia automatically using the Akruti English Phonetic layout. No software installation is required on your computer or mobile.
How do I type conjunct letters (ଯୁକ୍ତ ଅକ୍ଷର)?
Press the 'f' key as a Halant/linker between two consonants. Example: k+f+t = କ୍ତ. For Ya-Phala press Shift+Y after a consonant. For Ra-Phala press f+r. See our Finger Guide for full details.
Is this website completely free?
Yes, completely free! All 36 lessons across Beginner, Intermediate and Expert levels are free with no registration required. Your progress is saved automatically in your browser.
Can I use this on mobile?
Yes! Mobile users can type using their phone's Odia keyboard. Tap the typing box and your mobile keyboard will open. The website is fully responsive and works on all screen sizes.
What is the Akruti English Phonetic keyboard layout?
Akruti English Phonetic is the most popular Odia keyboard layout used by government offices, newspapers and professionals across Odisha. Each English key maps to an Odia character. Visit our Finger Guide page to learn the complete layout.
